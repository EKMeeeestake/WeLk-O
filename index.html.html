What is WeLk-O!?

WeLk-O! is a thrilling, strategic card game where players race to lower their hand's value and shout WeLk-O! before anyone else. With SlapBacks, Wild Utility Cards, and endless variation, it's built for gamers, casual players, and competitive spirits alike.

How to Play

  1. Start with 5 cards + 1 Wild Utility Card
  2. Play sets or runs to lower your hand
  3. Call WeLk-O! when your hand totals 5 or less — but be careful, you might get WeLk-O’d!
